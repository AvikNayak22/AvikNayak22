## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a Full Stack Developer based in India, passionate about building modern web applications that are as robust under the hood as they are beautiful on the screen. I specialize in the **Next.js** ecosystem, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. 

## 💡 My Coding Philosophy
> *"Code is read much more often than it is written."*

I strongly believe in writing clean, readable code with descriptive naming conventions. I'd rather spend an extra five minutes naming a variable perfectly than leave a confusing single-letter alias for my future self to decipher. Great Developer Experience (DX) isn't just for the end user—it applies to the codebase too!
